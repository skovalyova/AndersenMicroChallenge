511 San Francisco bay

  • San-Francisco Bay map with detailed transport information
  • Voice assistant on Spanish and English
  • Detailed route planning for all types of transport
  • San-Francisco Bay map with detailed transport information
  • Voice assistant on Spanish and English
  • Detailed route planning for all types of transport

Situation

San-Francisco Bay area is densely populated: almost 8 mln people live on a quite small territory. Developed transport network includes highways, suburb and city underground, railways. Except that, the number of personal and commercial transport constantly grows and that negatively impacts on the ecological situation of the region, also as worsens the traffic situation.

The united public transport organizations network of California decided to create mobile and web-service of transport help to the citizens ant tourists in San-Francisco Bay district. This initiative should reduce the transport burden and number of cars on the roads, and it should optimize the public transport network in this region. Besides, this service should positively affect the ecology and popularize bicycle riding.

Task

The client set us several global targets through our partner:

To develop a portal with interactive region map.

To integrate car sharing Carpool and Vanpool services.

To develop the informational voice service for navigation help.

To organize the work of road help service through the system.

Solution

Portal 511.org

Andersen specialists developed for the client a portal with interactive San-Francisco Bay map. Different road events are displayed on it in real time; the users can evaluate the road traffic and choose the optimal route. In addition, local citizens and tourists can map a route while using personal or city transport, or a bicycle.

This portal contains detailed information about regional roadside services, bicycle routes, weather forecast and emergency notifications about the events strongly affecting transport infrastructure. For example, any user can map a route for any kind of ground transport, taking into account closed roads, traffic jams, car accidents and roadside cafes available on the way.

Informational service

Along with the portal development we started to create interactive telephone service. Dialing a short number "511" the subscribers can without any operator's help get required information about the routes, nearest service stations, transport schedule, technical help call and other important for the travelers aspects.

Through the voice recognition system we realized the automatic switching to operator in case the system can't interpret the user's request. Taking into account the specificities of local population the system recognize the English and Spanish languages. As the first two months of service work showed, the voice assistant correctly processes more than 80% of request without any operator's help.

Help on roads

Anything can happen on the road, that's why local citizens and tourists of San-Francisco Bay should have a simple and clear access to help and service. And web-portal allows to call help in case of the car or bicycle break, if a user is lost or just wants to find the nearest diner or a hotel. All this is realized in the road help service developed by our team.

The information is available in automated mode on short number and on the website. Geolocation allows mapping a route promptly or direct help to any user in need. After the service launch during a short period of time hundreds of drivers got the tow trucks in time or were suggested a route to the nearest technical service station. The bicycle riders who were far from the civilization and faced some problems also received their help.

Integration of Carpool and Vanpool service

Another way to reduce the transport burden in the region is car sharing service. We added several popular specific services including our own 511.org, allowing users themselves to choose an appropriate variant with suitable terms.

Thus, the Carpool users can find a companion or a driver moving along the suitable route. The Vanpool service suggests to organize cargo transportations based on the principle of car sharing, when one car carries the goods of several clients located in one region. Users reduce the costs on goods transportation, and the number of trucks on the roads reduces too.

Result

Our specialists have been working on this project during one year. In the first 8 months after the service launched more than 150 thousand of local citizens and tourists of San-Francisco Bay region managed to use it. They got a new opportunity for automobile and bicycle navigation, for routes mapping using public transport, for prompt help receive and for car sharing.

The emergence of actively promoted alternative to personal transport gradually changes people's minds in San-Francisco Bay region. More than 5% of local population with the help of service chose public transport or car sharing over their personal one. Bicycle movement got a new impulse to develop. Thus, a detailed interactive guide of the region is doing its part to reduce the pollution level and protect the environment.
